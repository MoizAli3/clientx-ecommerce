Full client acquisition pipeline — from lead to signed contract.

Walk through each stage:

**Stage 1 — Lead Identification**
Ask: Who is the ideal client? (industry, size, location, budget range)
Output: List of 5-10 specific types of businesses to target

**Stage 2 — Outreach Message**
Generate a cold outreach message (email/LinkedIn DM):
- Subject: specific to their business (not generic)
- Opening: reference something specific about them
- Value prop: what problem you solve for THEM
- Social proof: one specific result
- CTA: low commitment ("15-minute call?")
- Under 100 words

**Stage 3 — Discovery Call Prep**
Generate questions to ask on the discovery call:
- What's their biggest technical challenge right now?
- What does success look like in 3 months?
- What's their timeline and budget range?
- Who else is involved in the decision?
- What have they tried before?

**Stage 4 — Proposal**
Run /estimate command to get numbers, then generate:
- Executive summary (their problem + your solution)
- Scope of work (what's included, what's not)
- Timeline with milestones
- Investment (price)
- Next steps

**Stage 5 — Follow-up**
Generate follow-up messages for days 3, 7, 14 if no response.
